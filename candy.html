DESCRIPTION OF HERSHEY'S

Hershey's Chocolate is a brand of chocolate that was founded in 1894 by Milton Hershey in Pennsylvania, USA. The company produces a variety of chocolate products, including chocolate bars, Kisses, and Reese's Peanut Butter Cups. Hershey's Chocolate is known for its creamy and smooth texture, and its signature taste, which comes from the use of milk chocolate. The company uses high-quality ingredients to create its chocolate products, which are loved by people all over the world. Hershey's Chocolate is committed to sustainability and ethical sourcing of its ingredients, and has initiatives in place to reduce its environmental impact. Overall, Hershey's Chocolate is a beloved brand that has been satisfying chocolate cravings for over a century.
